Rory McIlroy Driving Analysis

Rory McIlroy is currently the number one golfer in the world. He is considerably longer than the average PGA Tour Player and has generally increased his distance year over year.
The interseting thing is he is also considerably less accurate than the average golfer on tour, but is still the number one golfer in the world.
The argument can be made that the added distance is more important than an increase of accuracy as he is able to more easily hit greens in regulation when he is closer.
